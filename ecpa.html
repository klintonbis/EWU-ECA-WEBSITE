Welcome to EWU Club for Performing Arts

East West University Club for Performing Arts Established: 14 November, 2009 Since its inception, ECPA has built a reputation by successfully organizing some big events at the university premises even outside. ECPA got some talented and dedicated members including musicians, singers,actor/actress, reciters, dancers, designers and organizers who uphold the club with creativity & passion.
