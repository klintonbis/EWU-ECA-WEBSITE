Welcome to EWU Environmental and Social Club

East West University Environmental and Social Club was formed on 14 October, 1999. Since its inception, this club has been pursuing its moto, "SAVE LIFE , SAVE EARTH" through different environmental and social activities. It has been involved with social support programs like rehabilitation of the people in destitute, exploring the talents of the young generation, encouraging the youth in afforestation, community building, etc. for the last 24 years.
